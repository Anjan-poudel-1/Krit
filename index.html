About Me

Krit Kunwar Rana

My name is Krit, born and raised in Kathmandu. With over a decade spent in the fitness industry I know what being fit means to everyone. Fitness is hard to pursue if you don’t feel good about yourself and that’s where mental well-being comes in. As a counseling psychologist and a fitness advisor/trainer I aim at helping you achieve your fitness goals. I believe that when you FEEL GOOD, you excel in life. There's an undying drive in me that wants to help others find the perfect balance between fitness and happiness. I believe the pain of discipline is better than the pain of regret.
Revamp Your Lifestyle
A healthy lifestyle is much more than just lifting weights or showing up to the gym every day. It’s about finding your own groove and enjoying the process. It's about becoming a student of the game and learning every day. Let's face it; the process of learning never stops whether it be at the gym or school or in life.

Why Choose me?
With my degree in counseling psychology my workout plan not only covers all the domains of fitness but is also focused on a combination of both mental and physical well-being. As a beginner or as someone who wants to change the way they look or how they see themselves, I don’t have to try to put myself in your shoes. I have walked in your shoes and I can now help you turn your life around, too.
Custom training
Training programs that cover all the domains of fitness, tailor-made to suit your fitness goals. These workouts are not generic workouts programs, each individual gets a workout plan best suited for him/her.
Calculated Nutrition
Nutrition is the key force behind a healthy life, your body needs fuel to work and a perfectly laid out nutrition plan can give your body the boost it needs to energize your fitness journey.
Visible Results
When you feel good on the inside, it resonates. Your overall wellbeing will be reflected by your body on the outside. With a workout program that delivers all the goods, you can be rest assured that your transformation will be a very exciting journey, one that you will enjoy every single day.
Healthy Lifestyle
The key is to develop habits that will be with you for the rest of your life. Fitness is not only about looking good, but it’s about feeling good and staying healthy and happy. I will help you develop a healthy lifestyle.
Build A Better You
It takes drive, determination, motivation and consistency to succeed. Although it may not always be easy and you may face a few roadblocks along the way, you can be sure that you will eventually look at yourself and your body in a whole new light. All you need to do is take that first step on the journey to your new life.
